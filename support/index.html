Frequently Asked Questions

Does Veristate require an internet connection?

No, Veristate works completely offline. All your data is stored locally on your device. An internet connection is only needed if you choose to use cloud sync features.

How do I capture a 360¬∞ panorama?

Open an inventory, select a room, and tap "Capture Panorama". Follow the AR guide to capture 12 photos in a circle. The app will automatically stitch them into a panoramic view.

Can I add damage annotations after capturing?

Yes! Simply tap anywhere on the panorama to add an annotation. You can add a title, description, severity level, and even attach a detail photo.

How do digital signatures work?

At the end of an inventory, you can collect signatures from tenants, owners, or agents. They can sign directly on the device screen with their finger or Apple Pencil. Signatures are automatically timestamped.

What format are the reports?

Reports are generated as professional PDF documents that include property details, panoramic photos, damage annotations with photos, meter readings, and digital signatures.

How do I backup my data?

Your data is included in your iPhone's iCloud or iTunes backup. You can also export individual reports as PDF files and save them to Files, iCloud Drive, or share via email.

Can I delete all my data?

Yes. Go to Settings > Delete All Data. This will permanently remove all properties, inventories, photos, and reports from your device.

Is my data private?

Absolutely. Veristate stores all data locally on your device. We don't collect, transmit, or have access to any of your data. Your inventories, photos, and signatures remain completely private.
